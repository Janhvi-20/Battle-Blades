BLADE BATTLES

Game Objective

  • Defeat your opponent within the time limit 80 sec by using precise movements and strategic attacks.
  • Each player's health will be displayed over his head.
  • Player with more damage will lose else with equal damage the game will result in a draw.

Controls

  • Use the arrow keys to move your character horizontally and vertically across the arena.
  • Press the spacebar to execute a basic attack.
  • Both players should be facing eachother to cast damage on other.

Gameplay Mechanics

  • Each player starts with a set amount of health points (HP).
  • Dodging and blocking enemy attacks are essential for survival.
  • Timing is crucial, executing attacks at the right moment can deal more damage or counter your opponent's moves.
